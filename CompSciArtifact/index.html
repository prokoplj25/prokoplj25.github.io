Prosthetic limbs

What is a Prosthetic limb?

The definition of a prosthetic limb is a artificial devise that is used to replace a damage or missing body part.

The history of prostheic limbs

There has been many advancements in prosthetic limbs over the ages but we will be covering the major ones. The Story of prosthtic limbs goes all the way to around 950-710 B.C.E in Egypt were a noble women was found with a prosthetic big toe. The big toe allowed the women to be able to wear sandles which were the main footwear in Anceint Egypt at the time.
